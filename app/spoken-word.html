SPOKEN WORD

Writing and Performance Workshops + Cypher | Every Thursday 6PM-8:30PM at Blackspace Durham until April 13th 2017

Blackspace hosts weekly writing and performance workshops for teens of African descent every Thursday from 6:30PM - 8PM at our Durham studio. Youth aged 13-19 work on developing their writing skills, and performance techniques to take their work from the page to the stage! Poetry workshops are facilitated by program director/poet Mariah Monsanto as well as Destiny Hemphill and Yoni Jefferies. At the same time as the poetry workshops, youth interested in Hip Hop have the option to join a freestyle and theatre performance workshop, facilitated by emcee/founder of the UNC Cypher, J Rowdy.

Monthly Poetry Slams | Every 3rd Tuesday @ Beyu Caffe until March 21st, 2017 (Grand Slam Finals are April 7th at the Hayti Heritage Center)

Each month, on 3rd Tuesdays, workshops culminate in a monthly poetry slam at Beyu Caffe. Slams are free and open to the public. The mic is open to all youth between the ages of 13 and 19. Poetry slam winners are eligible to join Durham's first youth slam team, which will apply to compete at Brave New Voices.

Brave New Voices | July 19-22 2017 in the Bay Area

Now in its 20th year, Brave New Voices is an annual international youth poetry slam and festival, which convenes the most outstanding and outspoken youth poets from around the world for 5 days of workshops, slams, showcases, community service and civic participation events. Blackspace is applying to BNV and hopes to represent Durham with it's first youth poetry slam team at BNV 2017.
